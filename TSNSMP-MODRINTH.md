# TSNSMP

A whitelisted, community-driven survival SMP built for players who actually want to play — no client mods required.

---

## What is TSNSMP?

TSNSMP is a semi-vanilla survival SMP running a suite of server-side quality-of-life mods. Players join with a completely vanilla client and still get enhanced chat, tablist, live streaming indicators, custom name colors, and more — all handled server-side.

---

## Content Creator Friendly

TSNSMP has built-in support for streamers and content creators. Live status, clickable stream links, and real-time indicators are built right into the server experience — but you absolutely **don't** need to be a content creator to join and enjoy the server.

---

## No Mods Needed

All custom features are handled server-side. Join with a **standard vanilla Minecraft client** — no modpack, no setup, no hassle.

---
